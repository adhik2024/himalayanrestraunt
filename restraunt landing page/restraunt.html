Our Story

In the heart of the city, our restaurant is a passionate tribute to authentic Nepali cuisine. Our journey began with a vision to bring the vibrant flavors of Nepal to the world, wrapped in the warmth of our food-loving hearts.

We believe food is a celebration of culture and community, inviting you to join us on a culinary adventure. From the moment you step inside, our friendly staff will guide you through our menu, sharing stories and insights along the way. Come, be a part of our journey, as we share our passion for authentic Nepali food and transport your taste buds to the vibrant streets of Kathmandu, the serene tea gardens of Ilam, and the breathtaking beauty of the Himalayas. Experience the magic of Nepal, one delicious bite at a time, at our restaurant.
